ABOUT!




The Solution Circle Project is an interactive platform designed to enhance the communication and collaboration between students and teachers.
The project aims to address the challenges faced in traditional educational providing a comprehensive solution for effective student-teacher interactions.
The system allows students to track their grades, participate in classroom discussions, access study materials, and collaborate with peers.
Teachers, on the other hand, can manage assignments, provide feedback, communicate with students, and monitor their progress.
